NOVA VESHTI

Observing in my day to day life how materials and once loved possessions go to waste and contribute to pollution is something that upsets and frustrates me, so I started to collect scraps of jewellery from second hand shops and make new jewellery with it. In the spirit of full disclosure the products on this website are definitely not all plastic free and made of environmentally friendly materials nor are they of the highest quality, the purpose is to not let these materials go to waste or get disposed of improperly, they already exist so they might as well have another circle around
